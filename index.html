About

I am an undergraduate student at the Korea Advanced Institute of Science and Technology (KAIST), majoring in Mathematical Sciences and pursuing a double major in Computer Science.

My research interests lie in structural graph theory, graph algorithms, and parameterized complexity. I am particularly interested in graph decompositions, graph minors, width parameters, and algorithmic applications of structural graph theory.
